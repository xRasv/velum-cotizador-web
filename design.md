# Color Palette: Velum Architectural

This document outlines the refined color strategy used for the Velum Premium Invoice designs. The palette is designed to convey a high-end, modern, and architectural aesthetic.

## Primary Brand Color
The primary accent color is a vibrant, professional blue that draws attention to key information without being overwhelming.

- **Velum Blue:** `#1A6AFF`
  - *Usage:* Primary headings, totals, main borders, and key call-to-action buttons.

## Monochromatic Base
A sophisticated range of slates and grays provides the structural foundation, ensuring high readability and an "Apple-esque" minimalist feel.

- **Deep Slate (Ink):** `#0F172A` (Slate 900)
  - *Usage:* Main body text, primary labels, and dark UI elements.
- **Muted Slate:** `#64748B` (Slate 500)
  - *Usage:* Secondary information, captions, and inactive states.
- **Cool Gray (Subtle):** `#F8FAFC` (Slate 50)
  - *Usage:* Backgrounds, zebra striping for tables, and very subtle section dividers.

## Functional Colors
Used for status indicators and clean visual separation.

- **Background White:** `#FFFFFF`
  - *Usage:* Main page background to ensure perfect print quality.
- **Border/Divider:** `#E2E8F0` (Slate 200)
  - *Usage:* Thin, elegant lines for table rows and section separation.
- **Status: Awaiting Payment:** `#F5F3FF` (Subtle Purple Tint)
  - *Usage:* Status background highlights to provide a premium feel.

## Print Optimization
The palette is chosen to ensure that even when printed in grayscale, the contrast ratios remain high and the document remains professional and legible.
